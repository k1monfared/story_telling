# Catalog — by scenario

Use this index to find the right skill in the moment. Scenarios are organized by what you are doing, not by which book the skill came from. Each scenario lists ordered pointers to skills.

This catalog is updated during per-book finalization. New scenarios are added when needed; existing scenarios get more pointers as the library grows.

## Telling a story / writing fiction

### I'm drafting and don't know what comes next

- `saunders/follow-the-voice` — generate the next page from a voice seed and let the world emerge from vocabulary pressure rather than from a planned outline.
- `saunders/increasing-specification` — when the plot stalls because the character is too generic, add facts that rule out clichés until the character generates pressure for particular events.
- `saunders/iterative-preference-revision` — when you are stuck deciding the big questions before writing, react sentence-by-sentence and let the cumulative micro-decisions discover the shape.
- `saunders/escalation-by-vocabulary-constraint` — bypass the writer-self-image with a 200-word, 50-unique-word, 45-minute exercise that surfaces a register your habitual mode cannot reach.
- `saunders/energy-not-aspiration` — diagnose which voice actually generates a reader's energetic response so you know which voice to follow.
- `saunders/recipes/draft-by-energy-bypass` — the chain that runs energy probe, constraint bypass, sustain-the-voice, and per-decision revision in order.

### I'm revising a draft that feels flat

- `saunders/iterative-preference-revision` — start here, with the P/N meter and per-decision reactions across many passes.
- `saunders/heart-of-the-story` — name the central event the rest must serve, then audit each part against that center.
- `saunders/cornfeld-principle` — for each scene, ask whether it is entertaining and whether it advances the story in a non-trivial way.
- `saunders/truncation-test` — cut the ending or an internal unit to surface what it supplies that the surrounding prose lacks.
- `saunders/take-responsibility-for-every-element` — element-by-element audit of paragraphs and scenes, naming what each detail is doing.
- `saunders/respect-the-reader-cut` — at sentence and clause scope, cut stated reactions, restated context, and default detail the reader would supply.
- `saunders/causality-not-sequence` — convert "and then, and then" sequences into "and so" by installing causation between beats.
- `saunders/refuse-to-repeat-beats` — when the prose is sitting in a state that has already changed, find the next escalation or compress to it.
- `saunders/skip-the-meaningless-interval` — leap over real elapsed time the story has no work to do in.
- `saunders/ritual-banality-avoidance` — refuse the resolution the early pages have telegraphed, and let the harder second-order answer arrive.
- `saunders/two-version-comparison` — compose the inverted version of a craft choice and read what the actual version supplies.
- `saunders/holding-up-against-the-heart` — for static or apparently decorative elements, hold them next to the heart and read out the felt relation.
- `saunders/track-non-normative-excesses` — list the surface oddities and audit which ones the rest of the work redeems.
- `saunders/parameter-table-for-recurrence` — for repeated scene-types or relationships, tabulate the parameters and read across rows for the meaning each variation generates.
- `saunders/matrix-of-facts` — when prose drifts into editorialized or lyrical assertion, recast subjective claims in the same factual idiom as exteriors.
- `saunders/forced-cut-pace-discovery` — when you cannot articulate your editing sensibility, run the cut-to-quota or rank-among-versions exercise to surface what you cut for and what you protect.

### I want to know whether my story is working on a reader

- `saunders/the-basic-drill` — register honest located reactions to a finished read before allowing yourself to interpret.
- `saunders/page-break-diagnostic` — at chosen pauses mid-read, make the reader's evolving expectations visible by answering three questions.
- `saunders/truncation-test` — read the truncated version and feel what the actual ending must supply to convert narrative into story.
- `saunders/track-non-normative-excesses` — keep the TICHN cart while reading, then audit which oddities the rest of the work redeemed.

### I'm critiquing or workshopping someone else's draft

- `saunders/recipes/workshop-critique-of-a-draft` — the four-step workflow that runs the scope-by-scope audit in the right order.
- `saunders/ask-strategic-not-interpretive` — the disposition shift that re-poses interpretive questions as questions a writer can act on.
- `saunders/the-basic-drill` — collect honest located reactions before letting the conversation drift to interpretation.
- `saunders/hollywood-version` — agree on a one-sentence description of the story this draft wants to be before any change is proposed.
- `saunders/heart-of-the-story` — when the story has a known central event, name it as the destination the rest must serve.
- `saunders/cornfeld-principle` — test each structural unit on entertainment and on non-trivial advancement of the story.
- `saunders/take-responsibility-for-every-element` — for units that pass Cornfeld but feel almost-good, audit each element for stated function.
- `saunders/convert-moral-critique-to-technical` — when a moral concern is sticky and general, locate the inequitable narration that produced it and convert the concern into a revision instruction.

### I'm building a character

- `saunders/recipes/build-character-tolstoy-arc` — the four-step chain (specification, factual register, vantage rotation, redirected-energy climax) for a character whose arc culminates in transformation.
- `saunders/increasing-specification` — answer "which particular person is this" with facts that rule out clichés until plot becomes available.
- `saunders/matrix-of-facts` — render the character's exteriors and interiors in the same factual idiom so subjective claims produce involuntary belief.
- `saunders/mind-to-mind-vantage-shifts` — in morally loaded exchanges, rotate among public stance, private thought, and objective frame so the moral architecture is felt without being asserted.
- `saunders/transformation-by-redirected-energy` — render the climactic change as the same energy turned in a new direction, in the character's familiar idiom, with surprise arriving after the action.
- `saunders/panel-of-judges` — when the central event the character must land is unwitnessable (singing, a sermon, a kiss), build a graded panel whose reactions adjudicate it.
- `saunders/withhold-the-resolving-interior` — the contrast condition for the climax, when the prior pages have built two readings with equal force and entering the head would collapse them.

### I'm writing something with thematic charge without it becoming polemic

- `saunders/persistent-self-contradiction` — for each aspect that votes for the position, write in a counterweight that complicates without erasing it; recurse on each other-hand chain.
- `saunders/belief-by-character-attribution` — render the belief in full conviction, attribute it to a particular character, then plant a small concrete fact about the speaker that destabilizes the speech without refuting it.
- `saunders/withhold-the-resolving-interior` — at the climax, refuse the soliloquy that would announce the verdict; render only externals and let the question be irradiated rather than answered.
- `saunders/convert-moral-critique-to-technical` — when readers report the story endorses or condemns inequitably, audit parallel moments for narrative resources granted to one character and denied to another.

### I'm working with a strange premise or impossibility

- `saunders/psychological-physics-of-strangeness` — the meaning lives in the world's reactions to the impossibility, not in the impossibility itself; calibrate reactions until the rule they imply resembles a rule of our own world.
- `saunders/multiple-superimposed-weirdness` — compound rather than restate; ratchet up empty tiers (plan, world's resistance, narrator) so each adds a fresh category of weirdness.
- `saunders/improper-narrative-emphasis` — at sentence scope, deflect the literal mechanical question onto a replacement attribute that the world cares about.
- `saunders/skaz-narrator` — make the narrator a person whose graceless voice unconsciously reveals the system that produces him.
- `saunders/confess-the-oddness` — at the ending, have the prose acknowledge the irregularities without resolving them; the cart empties because the gaps have been seen.

### I'm trapped between the writer I am and the writer I want to be

- `saunders/energy-not-aspiration` — diagnose the trap by running the energy probe on your modes; commit to whichever produces energy rather than to the writer you wanted to be.
- `saunders/recipes/draft-by-energy-bypass` — the full sequence that diagnoses the trap, bypasses it in a single session, sustains the surfaced voice, and revises per-decision.
- `saunders/escalation-by-vocabulary-constraint` — bypass the writer-self-image with a constraint exercise that lets the playful mind surface a register your habitual mode keeps off-license.
- `saunders/forced-cut-pace-discovery` — surface your editing basis through forced operations on someone else's prose, then transfer to your own work.
- `saunders/is-it-helping` — apply the trial-balloon disposition to every craft model you currently hold; keep what is helping, let go of what is held only on prestige or correctness.

### I want to find or refine my voice

- `saunders/energy-not-aspiration` — locate the off-license mode whose readings produce energy rather than respect.
- `saunders/follow-the-voice` — sustain a voice from a seed and let the world emerge under vocabulary pressure.
- `saunders/escalation-by-vocabulary-constraint` — the constraint exercise that surfaces a playful register your writer-self-image keeps suppressed.
- `saunders/forced-cut-pace-discovery` — the cut-to-quota and rank-among-versions exercises that surface what you offend at and what you protect.
- `saunders/iterative-preference-revision` — apply your editing basis without defense or rationalization, refining the voice across hundreds of micro-decisions.

## Cross-cutting

### I'm trying to understand someone else's account

- `saunders/the-basic-drill` — before interpreting or rendering a verdict, locate your honest reactions to what you actually heard, in plain language, anchored to specific moments.
- `saunders/ask-strategic-not-interpretive` — re-pose interpretive questions about meaning as strategic questions about what each element of the account is doing, so the conversation can produce something actionable.

### My emotional reaction is in the way

- `saunders/the-basic-drill` — treat every honest reaction (including boredom, irritation, confusion) as valid data rather than as a failure to be hidden, and refuse the urge to skip past the felt response to a more sophisticated interpretation.
- `saunders/is-it-helping` — for any rule, model, or theory pulling you toward a verdict, apply the trial-balloon test: ask whether holding it is helping you reach what you actually want, not whether it is correct.
