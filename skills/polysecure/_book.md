Jessica Fern's *Polysecure* asks what attachment theory has to teach when you have multiple romantic partners. Her answer is the HEARTS framework — six moves that build secure attachment in any relationship structure — and the four-level "nested model" that situates the dyad inside family, community, and culture. Each chapter ends with a set of reflection prompts the reader is invited to take into their own relationships.

The skills in this library extract Fern's discrete moves: not "how to do nonmonogamy" but the underlying attachment work that any relationship configuration requires.
